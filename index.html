What is Rainy Dayz you ask!?

Rainy Dayz is an application used globally that will allow you to schedule events and/or activities near you and will track the weather of your upcomming events. The intended use is for the user to receive updates daily on any weather changes that may impact their upcoming event. If user cancels their event, based off incliment weather or not, use will be given suggestions of activities/events that are nearby.

Schedule your events and activities now and we will keep you up to date on the weather as your event day apporaches

Schedule an event now for a later date by clicking Schedule Event

Find events that are happening near you now or in the near/very near future by clicking Find My Location
